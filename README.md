# DdS_REFLOC 

Hello! Welcome to the REFLOC project page. REFLOC refers to "Recycling flows of concrete". In this project, we explored the French national concrete waste treatment dynamics during 2025 and 2095 using a combination of dynamic material flow analysis and life cycle analysis. The environmental impacts (climate change and particulate matter) are evaluated for the treatment of total concrete waste for the 10-year period. 
## Data source
  - Pauliuk, S., Heeren, N., Berrill, P. et al. Global scenarios of resource and emission savings from material efficiency in residential buildings and cars. Nat Commun 12, 5097 (2021). (https://doi.org/10.1038/s41467-021-25300-4)
  - PN RECYBETON (https://pnrecybeton.fr/)
  - PN FastCarb (https://fastcarb.fr/)
